Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

ConVibe ("ConVibe", "we", "us") helps fans follow their favorite celebrities and track which conventions those celebrities are appearing at. This Privacy Policy explains what information the ConVibe mobile app collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. ConVibe is operated by ConVibe LLC.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you create an account we collect your email address and authentication details, handled by our backend provider (Supabase).
  • Profile & preferences. The interests and sub-interests you select during onboarding, to personalize suggestions.
  • Your activity in the app. The celebrities and conventions you follow, and the "ConGoals" (photo-op and autograph plans) you create.
  • Feedback. If you send beta feedback, we collect your message along with the screen you were on and the app version, to help us fix issues.
  • Push tokens. If you enable notifications, a device push token so we can send appearance alerts. You can disable this anytime in your device settings.
  • Basic technical data. Standard information needed to operate the service (e.g., device platform and app version).

ConVibe is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

How we use your information

  • To provide core features — following celebrities, showing convention appearances, and tracking your ConGoals.
  • To personalize the celebrities and conventions we surface to you.
  • To send you alerts about appearances you've chosen to follow (only if you enable notifications).
  • To diagnose problems and improve the app.

How information is shared

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that operate the app on our behalf, and only as needed:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and storage hosting.
  • Apple — app distribution and, during beta, TestFlight.

Celebrity and convention catalog data (including images and filmographies) is sourced from public and third-party sources such as The Movie Database (TMDB); this is reference content shown in the app, not your personal information. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Data retention & your choices

  • We keep your account data for as long as your account is active.
  • You can stop notifications anytime in your device settings.
  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to safeguard your data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above when we do.

Contact us
